# Bright-Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis
In today’s competitive retail environment, data-driven decision-making plays a critical role in improving business performance and maintaining a competitive advantage. Organizations are increasingly relying on data analytics to better understand customer behavior, optimize operations, and drive revenue growth. This project focuses on the analysis of transactional sales data from Bright Coffee Shop, a business seeking to enhance its overall performance under the leadership of a newly appointed Chief Executive Officer (CEO). The dataset provided contains detailed information on daily sales activities, including product categories, individual product sales, transaction quantities, time intervals, and revenue generated across different store locations. The purpose of this analysis is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that can support strategic decision-making. By examining patterns in sales performance, product demand, and customer purchasing behavior, the project aims to identify key drivers of revenue and areas that require improvement. Furthermore, this analysis explores how sales vary across different time periods, such as hours of the day, days of the week, and months of the year. Understanding these trends is essential for optimizing staffing, inventory management, and marketing strategies. Additionally, evaluating product-level performance allows the business to focus on high-performing items while addressing underperforming products. Ultimately, the findings from this project will provide actionable recommendations to help Bright Coffee Shop increase revenue, improve operational efficiency, and better meet customer demand. This analysis demonstrates the value of leveraging data analytics as a tool for informed decision-making and sustainable business growth.
